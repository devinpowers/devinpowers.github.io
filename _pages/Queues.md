Time Complexity of a Queue

| Operation | Running Time (Average) | Running Time (Worst) |
|-----------|------------------------|----------------------|
| Access    | O (n)                  | O (n)                |
| Search    | O (n)                  | O (n)                |
| Insertion | O (1)                  | O (1)                |
| Deletion  | O (1)                  | O (1)                |
